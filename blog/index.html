Helping Tor Take Back the Internet

Who I Am

I'm Nicolei Ocana. The core values that best reflect my identity are community, responsibility, and meaningful work. I poke fun of my 38 years of age; all jest aside, my experiences contributed to how I personify these core values.

The characteristic of community is important because all individuals deserve an equal chance. There are matters that are out of one's control negatively affecting their education, their right to stand up for what they believe in, and informing themselves of what is going on outside of where they live. If I had a chance to bring the community together by offering a resolution to these issues, I would. I cannot be a passive participant while there exists some who are less fortnuate than I am only because they were born in an oppressive environment.

As a representative of the older generation, I feel it is important to exhibit charity to help those who are in the same position as I once was: confused and overwhelmed. Web development can be outrageously difficult without the proper guidance. Responsibility is exemplified whenever I have the opportunity to help anyone with a "noob" question. No one should have to feel threatened when they are simply looking for direction when developing their technological passion. I'm glad to patiently offer my support when someone is stuck; I remember being at that point once and it is my responsibility to offer the same mentorship that I once received.

A job well done is its own reward. Demonstrating meaningful work is projected from volunteering my time, knowing that financial compensation truly isn't the movitating factor to complete any task to the best of my abilities. Any responsibility is met with the same enthusiasm and diligence.

My motivation to apply to Outreachy is to serve as an example that no matter your age, your educational background, or hardships you face in life, anyone has a chance to achieve their goals. All the hard work, sacrifice, and perseverance will result in accomplishment. Believe in yourself and never give up.
